Help - B◇xDrum

Getting Started

How to Play: Tap on any drum pad to play the corresponding sound. The app supports multitouch so you can play multiple sounds at once!

Key Features

  • Volume Control: Use the slider in the settings to adjust the sound level.
  • Metronome: Turn it on/off with the toggle. Adjust the beats per minute (BPM) using the slider.
  • Recording: Press the microphone button to start recording your session, then press stop to save.
  • Reverb Control: Use the reverb slider in settings to control the amount of reverb applied to the sound.

Interactive Features

Adjust Settings: Go to the settings (gear icon) to adjust the volume, reverb, or metronome settings.
